Starknet Roadmap 2026: S-two and STRK20 Privacy Audit

🗓️ 2026-04-07 🔥 Trending: Starknet, S-two prover, STRK20 protocol, Celestia Blobstream, RedStone oracle

Starknet’s 2026 roadmap reads like a technical sprint, not a marketing sprint. The network has already slashed proving costs with the S‑two prover rollout in Q4 2025, and March 31 2026 saw the STRK20 privacy protocol go live, delivering native shielded balances. What follows is a hard‑look at the concrete upgrades, the latency gains from preconfirmations.

S‑two Prover Deployment


The S‑two prover, deployed on mainnet in Q4 2025, replaces the original recursive STARK prover with a batch‑optimized circuit that reduces gas consumption per proof by roughly 40 % [Per Official Starknet Roadmap]. This shift directly translates into lower transaction fees for end users, as the fee model is tightly coupled to proof size. The upgrade also unlocks the network’s 10k TPS target by freeing up calldata slots on Ethereum’s EIP‑4844 blob space, which Starknet continues to use for data availability [Per Starknet Technical Specs].

STRK20 Privacy Protocol Launch


On 2026‑03‑31, Starknet introduced STRK20, a privacy layer that leverages zero‑knowledge STARKs to encrypt balances at the protocol level [Per Official Starknet Roadmap]. Unlike layer‑2 mixers that rely on off‑chain relayers, STRK20 integrates directly into the rollup’s state transition, ensuring that shielded amounts are indistinguishable from public ones without sacrificing L1 finality.


The protocol’s design mirrors the “validity‑rollup” paradigm: a succinct proof attests to the correctness of both public and private state changes before being posted to Ethereum.

Preconfirmations


Preconfirmations introduced in April 2026 achieve sub‑1 s latency for 95 % of transactions [Verified via Starknet Docs].

Key Takeaways


Starknet’s 2026 milestones solidify its position as a high‑throughput, privacy‑first validity rollup.



    • S‑two prover cuts proof‑generation costs by ~40 % and reinforces the 10k TPS ambition [Per Official Starknet Roadmap].

    • STRK20 delivers on‑chain shielded balances [Per Official Starknet Roadmap].

    • Preconfirmations introduced in April 2026 achieve sub‑1 s latency for 95 % of transactions [Verified via Starknet Docs].
