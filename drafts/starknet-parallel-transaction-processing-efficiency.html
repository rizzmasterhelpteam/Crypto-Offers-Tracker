Why Speed Matters

Imagine you’re buying a breakout token right as it spikes. On a congested chain, the order can sit pending, giving the price a chance to reverse and erasing the upside you chased. Faster settlement locks in the price you saw, turning a potential miss into a clean win.

Beyond slippage, speed cuts the “opportunity cost” of idle capital. When your funds sit in a pending transaction, they can’t be redeployed elsewhere. Parallel processing means your capital is back in your pocket—ready for the next move—within seconds, not minutes.

For the everyday trader, that translates to more trades per day, tighter control over entry points, and a healthier bottom line. It’s the difference between watching a race from the sidelines and being in the driver’s seat.

Starknet’s Parallel Engine

Starknet leverages a STARK-based proof system that bundles dozens of transactions into a single, cheap proof. This approach allows for efficient processing of multiple transactions.

Coupled with preconfirmations, the network pushes a tentative receipt to your wallet in under a second, while the final proof settles later. You see the trade instantly, and the security of the underlying network still backs it.

Real-world numbers back the claim: Starknet processes roughly 4,500 transactions per second, and the fee per transaction averages $0.45. The parallel design also means the network can absorb sudden volume spikes without choking, keeping your trades flowing.
