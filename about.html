About Us

Our Mission

Crypto Offers Tracker was built to solve a simple problem: the crypto landscape is constantly changing, and it's hard to keep track of the best airdrops, staking yields, and trading bonuses across dozens of platforms.

Our goal is to aggregate the most current and lucrative crypto offers in one place, saving you time and helping you maximize your crypto journey.

How It Works

We use advanced AI combined with real-time web search to scan official exchanges and DeFi protocols twice a day. This ensures the offers you see are fresh, accurate, and link directly to the official sources.

Supported Platforms

We track over 20 top-tier platforms, including Binance, OKX, Bybit, Coinbase, KuCoin, Kraken, and major decentralized exchanges like Uniswap and PancakeSwap.
