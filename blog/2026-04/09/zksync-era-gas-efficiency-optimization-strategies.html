1. Understanding Paymasters: Pay Gas Without ETH

zkSync Era’s native Paymaster system lets any smart contract (or dApp-integrated one) sponsor or convert your gas fees into the token you already hold. This eliminates the annoying need to keep a tiny ETH balance just for fees.

How it works in practice (2026):

  1. Connect a compatible wallet (Holdstation recommended for best AA support).
  2. On supported DEXs like SyncSwap or Mute.io, look for the “Pay with” or gas token selector during the transaction.
  3. Choose USDC or USDT — the Paymaster automatically handles approval and conversion.
  4. Confirm once. No separate ETH swap required.

Popular integrations in 2026 include SyncSwap’s built-in Paymaster (often enables near-zero effective gas for swaps) and third-party solutions like ZyFi for broader dApp coverage.

2. Account Abstraction: Smart Batching & Better UX

Unlike Ethereum’s ERC-4337 (which is still add-on), zkSync Era has **native** Account Abstraction at the protocol level. Your account behaves like a smart contract, enabling:

  • One-click batching of approve + swap + liquidity add
  • Custom rules (session keys, spending limits — supported in advanced wallets)
  • Smoother experience that feels closer to a centralized exchange

Recommended wallets in 2026:

  • Holdstation — Best for native AA and Paymaster demos; excellent mobile + extension support.
  • Other EVM wallets (MetaMask, Rabby) work but may require manual Paymaster selection for full benefits.
